Our Programs & Services

Green Future Initiative offers a variety of programs and services to promote environmental education and sustainable practices in communities across South Africa.

Community Tree Planting

Join our weekly tree planting initiatives across South African communities. We provide all the tools and training needed.

  • Native tree species planting
  • Community engagement workshops
  • Maintenance and care training

School Workshops

Our environmental education program reaches over 50 township schools with engaging, curriculum-aligned content.

  • Environmental science lessons
  • Hands-on planting activities
  • Teacher training programs
  • Educational resource development
Request a Workshop

Tree-A-Grandparent Program

This innovative program pairs orphans with elders to plant fruit trees at care homes, creating intergenerational bonds.

  • Intergenerational community building
  • Fruit tree planting and maintenance
  • Nutrition education
